Explore Dubai

Dubai is unlike anywhere else on the planet. With its bold architecture and audacious style, the United Arab Emirates’ largest city is a distinct fusion of its Bedouin heritage and an ultramodern style all its own. Dubai doesn’t just live up to its reputation; it will completely exceed your expectations. Explore the glitz and glam, and then uncover the ancient traditions of this multifaceted city. Plan of Actions

ITI N ERARY 04 N /05 D

Day 01 – DUBAI AIRPORT -HOTEL -MARINA CRUISE

Reach airport and our representative driver will pick you up and drop you at your respective hotel. Check-in and relax for the day In the evening proceeds to Marina Cruise with dinner and live shows Admire the illuminated skyscrapers from the deck of your cruise as you feast on an International buffet. Later you will be dropped at your hotel Overnight at the hotel in Dubai.

Day 02 –DUBAI HALF-DAY CITY TOUR -THE DUBAI MALL - BURJ KHALIFA

Post breakfast at the hotel, proceed to Half day City Tour of Dubai with Burj Al Arab, Jumeriah Beach is followed by the Gold Souk market. Gold souk market is a traditional & Dubai Frame styled street market situated in Deira which happens to be the older part of Dubai city. then Proceeds for Dubai Frame After that Dubai Mall, Fountain Show, and Burj Khalifa. The Dubai mall is the world's largest mall in terms of land area and is considered the epicenter of luxury shops and stores for the finest shopping experience in Dubai. After Dubai mall, We will visit the 124th floor of Burj Khalifa. The view from here will give a glimpse of the ultra-modern development of this sophisticated city After that enjoy the overwhelming Dubai fountain show return Back to Hotel overnight stay in Dubai.

Day 03 –DESERT SAFARI WITH BBQ DINNER

After Breakfast, proceed to Desert Safari with BBQ Dinner. Our expert driver will take you to the sands of the Lahbab desert and ride over the dunes for an adventurous 4x4 experience. Later head to the desert camp. Enjoy the entertaining live shows where dancers perform a belly dance show, Tanoura dance show, and a fire dance. Satiate yourself with a delicious buffet meal and a BBQ. Later return to your hotel for an overnight stay in Dubai

Day 04 –Day Free for Leisure

After Breakfast, Full Day at leisure explore Dubai City on own an overnight stay at hotel.

Day 05 - HOTEL - DUBAI AIRPORT

After Breakfast, Check out from the hotel. Transfer to the airport for your flight back to India. Dubai will miss you till you visit again!

Stay in Vietnam Options

Vietnam, a captivating Southeast Asian nation, offers a rich tapestry of history, culture, and natural beauty. Located on the easternmost part of the Indochinese Peninsula, Vietnam is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, bustling cities, and warm hospitality. One of Vietnam's most iconic destinations is the enchanting Halong Bay, a UNESCO World Heritage site. With its emerald-green waters and towering limestone karsts jutting out of the sea, Halong Bay is a true marvel of nature. Exploring this ethereal landscape on a traditional junk boat is an unforgettable experience.
