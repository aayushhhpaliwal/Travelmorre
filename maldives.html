Plan of Actions

ITINERARY 05 N /06 D

Day 1: Arrive at Male

Meet our representative, and transfer via Speed Boat/ Seaplane to your resort. Check-in to your resort. Enjoy lunch. Spend the day relaxing and admiring the views of crystal clear waters ebbing sandy beaches. Dinner and Overnight in the Resort.

Day 02 – Beach Bliss

Enjoy a hearty breakfast at the resort. Enjoy the luxury of Maldivian hospitality with a delicious lunch. Explore the sunkissed beaches of Maldives. Dinner at the comfort of the Resort. Overnight in the Resort.

Day 03 - Serene Escape

Wake up to a lazy morning. Enjoy your breakfast at the resort. Relax for some time and day at leisure. Dinner at the comfort of the Resort. Overnight in the Resort.

Day 04 - Maldives Activities

After breakfast at the resort. Explore the Property & Indulge in your favorite activities full day at leisure. Dinner at the comfort of the Resort. Overnight in the Resort.

Day 05 - Free for Leisure

After breakfast proceeds to the airport by Speedboat. Fly back to India with amazing memories!
